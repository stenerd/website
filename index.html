How does it work ?

+

Its super easy , Sign up with your email , Choose what you want to collect payments for . Create a link and send to the users that need to make payments .

Can I create different payment links on one account ?

+

Yes sure you can , You can create multiple payment links for different products. from contributions for special occasions to fee collection for what you want to

Are there fees ?

+

We charge a 3% fee on each transaction . which is paid by the users as they make payments , Ensuring that you get your payment in full.

What payments do you accept ?

+

We collaborate with the best payment providers in Africa to make payments easy, Card Payments , Bank Transfers , USSD , and more

Will anybody have access to my links?

+

We offer private and public links . Only people that you send the links can pay . However, Private links allow you to create a link and upload a CSV file containing the specific users who need to pay. This safeguards the link from unauthorized users even if they have the link.
